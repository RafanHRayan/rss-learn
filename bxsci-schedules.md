## **Test Days**

**Monday —** Sciences[^1]

**Tuesday —** English, [World Languages](#user-content-fn-2)[^2], and Arts[^3]

**Wednesday —** Mathematics[^4] and Health

**Thursday —** [Social Studies](#user-content-fn-5)[^5]

**Friday —** Sciences (Same as Monday)

[^1]: i.e. biology, chemistry, computer science, science research, etc.

[^2]: Spanish, french, italian, chinese, japanese, and latin.

[^3]: i.e. visual art, music, chorus, band, etc.

[^4]: i.e. geometry, algebra, precalculus, calculus, etc.

[^5]: i.e. global history, world history, etc.
