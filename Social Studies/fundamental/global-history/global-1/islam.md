# Islam

## Linguistic Roots of Islam

The root is S-L-M, and can be interpreted into two: Islam and Salam. They captured the unified meaning of peace through primary submission.

Islam is to attain true peace in this life and the next by primarily submitting one's will to the will of God. The connection isn't just linguistic, but also spiritual.

## The Arabian Peninsula

The Arabian Peninsula was located in the dead center of Europe, Asia, and Africa, giving it easy access to different trade routes.

Before Islam, Arabians were mostly polytheistic and Arabia wasn't under a sing government.

### Mecca

The Quraysh was a group of people that controlled Mecca, which was the wealthiest trade city in Arabia at the time.

#### The Kaaba

The Kaaba was one of the holiest places in the world at its time. Before Islam, it was a place where anyone of [any religion](#user-content-fn-1)[^1] could go to practice their religion. The Kaaba had statues of Polytheistic gods to main inclusivity.

Since the Quraysh had access to the Kaaba, they were able to tax visitors[^2] to gain profit.

## Muhammadﷺ

Muhammadﷺ was born in 570 CE and was said to be the Seal of Prophethood. He was said to be the final messenger of God. He quickly gained a positive reputation as [The Trustworthy](#user-content-fn-3)[^3].

### The First Revelation

In 610 CE, Muhammadﷺ was meditating in the Cave of Hira until he was given his first revelation. He was visited by the angel Gabriel and told to recite, where he was given the revelation in Arabic. Muhammadﷺ would go on to be given many different revelations from 610 CE-632 CE, which later forms the Quran.

{% hint style="success" icon="lightbulb" %}
Muhammadﷺ did not know how to write, so he had employed scribes to transcribe the revelations and messages he received as they were revealed to him.
{% endhint %}

## The Quran

The Quran is the holy text of Islam, written in Arabic and composed in poetry form. In contrast to the Bible or Torah, the Quran is said to be the word of God.
