# Cellular Respiration and Photosynthesis

## Cellular Respiration

After obtaining glucose, cells need to convert them into energy that it can use. To do this, they use **cellular respiration**.

Cellular respiration is the process where cells break down nutrients to make usable energy.

## Review

Energy is the ability to do work. It is essentially the capacity to do change.

### Aerobic vs. Anaerobic

Aerobic cellular respiration is performed by the mitochondria and requires oxygen. Anaerobic cellular respiration is performed in the cytoplasm and doesn't require oxygen, though it creates a significantly lower amount of ATP.
