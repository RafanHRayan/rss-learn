<details>

<summary><i class="fa-triple-chevrons-right">:triple-chevrons-right:</i> Helicopter Kick</summary>

**Perform** — Jump in the air and kick on opposite sides.

{% hint style="success" icon="fire-flame-curved" %}
Works best when two opponents are on either side of you.
{% endhint %}

</details>

<details>

<summary><i class="fa-head-side-gear">:head-side-gear:</i> Pure Parry</summary>

**Definition** — A pure parry is a serious of quick, sharp moves that is to channel the opponent's attack in order to deliver a tangible, yet overwhelming counterattack.

</details>

<details>

<summary><i class="fa-sparkles">:sparkles:</i> "Rise and Serve"</summary>

**Perform** — Upon an advancing opponent, strike their chin with your elbow (in an upwards motion) and follow through with a strike with [the other hand](#user-content-fn-1)[^1] to the chest, sending them flying back in the direction they came from.

**Bonus** — Whilst elbow striking, pure parry the opponent's initial attack and channel this energy for a more powerful chest strike.

{% hint style="success" icon="fire-flame-curved" %}
Use your dominant hand to perform the strike to the chest.
{% endhint %}

</details>

[^1]: The other hand of that which was used to perform the elbow strike.
